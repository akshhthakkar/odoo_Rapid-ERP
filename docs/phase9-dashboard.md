# Phase 9: Executive KPI Dashboard Specifications

This document outlines the metrics aggregation, card structures, charts, recent activities feed, alert systems, and analytical frameworks implemented in Phase 9 for the Executive Dashboard.

---

## Endpoints

All dashboard operations are served under the `/api/dashboard` endpoint and require session token authentication.

| Method | Route | Access Role | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| `GET` | `/api/dashboard` | `ADMIN`, `BUSINESS_OWNER`, `INVENTORY_MANAGER` | Computes live KPIs, trends, recent audit feeds, stock level alerts, and timeline values. Scoped to `tenantId`. |

---

## Dashboard Metrics & Aggregations

The dashboard queries and aggregates stats across all business layers. Key metrics returned in the JSON payload include:

### 1. Sales Command Center
* **Sales Count Today**: Total number of Sales Orders confirmed today.
* **Pending Deliveries**: Confirmed Sales Orders waiting to be shipped out.
* **Partially Delivered**: Orders that have had some quantities shipped but remain open.
* **Cancelled Orders**: Total cancelled Sales Orders in the date range.
* **Total Sales Revenue**: Cumulative dollar revenue generated by confirmed sales.
* **Revenue Trend**: Month-over-month (MoM) revenue percentage change.

### 2. Procurement Command Center
* **Open Purchase Orders**: Active draft or sent POs waiting for goods receipt.
* **Partially Received**: POs that have had partial vendor shipments registered.
* **Delayed POs**: Active POs whose expected delivery date is in the past.
* **Pending Replenishments**: Shortage lines in Sales Orders waiting on active MTO purchase generation.

### 3. Manufacturing Command Center
* **Active Manufacturing Orders**: MOs currently in confirmed or in-progress states.
* **Completed MOs**: Finished production runs within the date range.
* **Delayed MOs**: Active MOs whose scheduled completion date is in the past.
* **Work Orders In-Progress**: Count of individual work center stations actively executing assembly steps.

### 4. Recent Activities Feed
* Returns the last 10 entries from the `AuditLog` table.
* Includes: performing user's name, action type, text description, and timestamp.

### 5. Stock Level Alerts
* **Low Stock Alert**: Queries products whose on-hand quantity is below 10 units.
* **High Stock Volume Warning**: Flags products whose on-hand quantity has exceeded a high threshold, indicating potential warehouse capacity blockages.

---

## Interactive Filters

The dashboard calculations support two date parameters:
1. **`startDate`**: Bounds the starting timeframe (defaults to 30 days ago).
2. **`endDate`**: Bounds the ending timeframe (defaults to now).

When these query parameters are passed, the dashboard dynamically filters all aggregations and trend computations to the specified date range.

---

## Security & Scopes

1. **Role Gating**: Access is restricted to `ADMIN`, `BUSINESS_OWNER`, and `INVENTORY_MANAGER` roles. Other roles (such as `SALES_USER` or `PURCHASE_USER`) will receive a `403 Forbidden` block and be redirected to their role-specific landing views.
2. **Multi-Tenancy Guard**: All database queries are filtered by matching user's `tenantId`, ensuring complete isolation. A user can never view metrics or activity history belonging to other companies.
